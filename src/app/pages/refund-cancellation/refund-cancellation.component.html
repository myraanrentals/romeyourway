Refund & Cancellation Policy

We value your trust and strive to provide a seamless experience. Please review our policy regarding refunds, cancellations, and rescheduling:

1. Cancellation by Customer

  • Within 48 hours of scheduled service:
    • No refund of the advance amount will be provided, regardless of whether the cancellation is informed or uninformed.
  • 72 hours or more before scheduled service:
    • If the cancellation is informed, the customer will receive a coupon code equivalent to the advance amount, valid for future bookings and adjustable against the new booking amount only.
    • Uninformed cancellations (no prior communication) will forfeit the advance amount.

2. Refund Eligibility

  • A full refund will be processed only if the company fails to deliver the service due to any circumstances.

3. Rescheduling Policy

  • Customers may reschedule their booking if informed at least 48 hours before the scheduled service time, subject to availability.
  • Rescheduling requests made within 48 hours may incur additional charges or be treated as a cancellation (no refund).

4. Payment & Dispute Terms

  • If a customer pays the balance amount via PhonePay (Credit/Debit Card, PayPal, etc.), it implies:
    • ✅ Service was rendered as agreed.
    • ✅ No unresolved issues exist.
    • ✅ Balance payment = Acknowledgment of satisfactory service.
    • ✅ Customer agrees not to raise disputes for paid amounts in the future.
  • NOTE: To qualify for a refund of any advance or booking payment via PhonePay, submit a written request to care@romeyourway.com on the same day as the scheduled service.
  • False Dispute Penalty:
    • Liable for transaction fees + 15% penalty.
    • Blacklisted from future bookings until resolved.

5. General Terms

  • Refunds are processed in 10 business days.
  • Coupons issued have no expiry date.
  • The company reserves the right to modify this policy with prior notice.
